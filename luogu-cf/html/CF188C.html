LCM【卡语言已经被隐藏】

题意翻译

输入两个数,求这两个数的最小公倍数

题目描述

Least common multiple (LCM) of two numbers is the smallest positive integer which is divisible by both of them. You are given integers $ a $ and $ b $ . Calculate their LCM.

输入输出格式

输入格式


The input contains two integers $ a $ and $ b $ ( $ 1<=a,b<=10^{3} $ ), separated by a single space.

输出格式


Output LCM( $ a $ , $ b $ ).

输入输出样例

输入样例 #1

10 42

输出样例 #1

210

输入样例 #2

123 41

输出样例 #2

123
